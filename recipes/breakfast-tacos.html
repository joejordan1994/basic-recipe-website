Breakfast Tacos

The best breakfast there ever was, the best breakfast there ever will be.

Description

Breakfast tacos represent an exquisite morning indulgence, where soft tortillas envelop a medley of fluffy scrambled eggs, crisped bacon or savory chorizo, and slices of creamy avocado. A sprinkle of aged cheddar or queso fresco introduces a touch of richness, while a drizzle of vibrant salsa or a dollop of sour cream adds a refreshing contrast. Each bite weaves together the simplicity of ingredients with a complexity of flavors, crafting a delightful symphony that awakens the senses and celebrates the dawn of a new day.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 Soft flour or corn tortillas
  • 6 Eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 pound cooked and crumbled bacon
  • 1 sliced avocado
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • A handful of fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • Salsa verde
  • Sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for cooking the eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the Meat cook it in a skillet over medium heat until crisp. Drain on paper towels and then crumble or chop.
  2. Scramble the Eggs In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat, add a bit of butter or oil, and pour in the eggs. Gently scramble the eggs, stirring occasionally, until they are softly set. Remove from heat.
  3. Warm Tortillas Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side or until warm and pliable. Alternatively, wrap them in a damp cloth and microwave for 20-30 seconds.
  4. Mix in Vegetables Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes, just until they start to soften. Remove from heat.
  5. Combine Creamy Elements In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the condensed cream of mushroom soup and sour cream until smooth.
  6. Combine Meat Mixture with Sauce Add the cooked meat and vegetable mixture to the bowl with the cream of mushroom and sour cream mixture. Stir until all ingredients are well combined.
  7. Layer the Casserole In a large baking dish, spread the meat and vegetable mixture evenly on the bottom. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese over the mixture.
  8. Add Tater Tots Arrange the frozen tater tots in a single layer on top of the cheese. For a more refined appearance, line them up neatly in rows.
  9. Bake Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for about 25 minutes, or until the tater tots are golden and crispy.
  10. Add Final Cheese Layer Sprinkle the remaining cup of cheddar cheese over the tater tots and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Garnish Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle with fresh herbs if using.
  12. Serve Scoop portions onto plates, ensuring each has a share of the crispy tots, creamy middle layer, and meaty base.
