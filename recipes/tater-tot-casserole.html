Tater Tot Casserole

The best there ever was, the best there ever will be. Period.

Description

Tater Tot Casserole, an epitome of comfort cuisine, marries the rustic charm of seasoned ground meat with a vibrant array of vegetables, each contributing its distinct flavor. This foundation is crowned with a layer of golden, crispy tater tots, offering a delightful textural contrast. Enveloped in a creamy, rich sauce and topped with a melted, artisanal cheese crust, the casserole becomes a symphony of flavors and textures, transforming the humble ingredients into a dish of elevated culinary elegance. Each bite offers a warm embrace of nostalgia and sophistication, making it a cherished masterpiece on any table.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 package (32 oz) frozen tater tots
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Fresh herbs (such as parsley or chives)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) to ensure it's ready for baking.
  2. Brown the Ground Beef In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef, breaking it apart with a spoon, until it's no longer pink. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add Aromatics Stir in the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion is translucent and aromatic, about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Mix in Vegetables Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes, just until they start to soften. Remove from heat.
  5. Combine Creamy Elements In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the condensed cream of mushroom soup and sour cream until smooth.
  6. Combine Meat Mixture with Sauce Add the cooked meat and vegetable mixture to the bowl with the cream of mushroom and sour cream mixture. Stir until all ingredients are well combined.
  7. Layer the Casserole In a large baking dish, spread the meat and vegetable mixture evenly on the bottom. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese over the mixture.
  8. Add Tater Tots Arrange the frozen tater tots in a single layer on top of the cheese. For a more refined appearance, line them up neatly in rows.
  9. Bake Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for about 25 minutes, or until the tater tots are golden and crispy.
  10. Add Final Cheese Layer Sprinkle the remaining cup of cheddar cheese over the tater tots and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Garnish Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle with fresh herbs if using.
  12. Serve Scoop portions onto plates, ensuring each has a share of the crispy tots, creamy middle layer, and meaty base.
