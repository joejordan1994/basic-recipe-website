Breakfast Tacos

The best breakfast there ever was, the best breakfast there ever will be.

Description

Breakfast tacos represent an exquisite morning indulgence, where soft tortillas envelop a medley of fluffy scrambled eggs, crisped bacon or savory chorizo, and slices of creamy avocado. A sprinkle of aged cheddar or queso fresco introduces a touch of richness, while a drizzle of vibrant salsa or a dollop of sour cream adds a refreshing contrast. Each bite weaves together the simplicity of ingredients with a complexity of flavors, crafting a delightful symphony that awakens the senses and celebrates the dawn of a new day.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 Soft flour or corn tortillas
  • 6 Eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 pound cooked and crumbled bacon
  • 1 sliced avocado
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • A handful of fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • Salsa verde
  • Sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for cooking the eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat in a skillet over medium heat until crisp. Drain on paper towels and then crumble or chop.
  2. Scramble the eggs in a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat, add a bit of butter or oil, and pour in the eggs. Gently scramble the eggs, stirring occasionally, until they are softly set. Remove from heat.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side or until warm and pliable. Alternatively, wrap them in a damp cloth and microwave for 20-30 seconds.
  4. Fill the tortillas on a flat surface. Spoon the scrambled eggs evenly among the tortillas.
  5. Add the toppings to the eggs with the cooked bacon or chorizo, slices of avocado, and a sprinkle of cheese.
  6. Garnish with a dollop of sour cream and a drizzle of salsa on top. If desired, garnish with fresh cilantro or chives for an added burst of flavor and color.
  7. Enjoy immediately while they are warm and fresh, with additional salsa and sour cream on the side for extra flavor.
